WEB이란 무엇인가?

The "web," short for the World Wide Web (WWW), is a vast system of interconnected documents (webpages) with text, images, and other media, accessed via the Internet using browsers like Chrome or Firefox to navigate hyperlinks (URLs) to find information, entertainment, and services, fundamentally different from the Internet itself (the underlying network). It's a global information space where servers deliver content to users' devices, enabling online activities from browsing to complex applications, built on open standards like HTML, HTTP, and CSS.
